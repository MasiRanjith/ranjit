ABOUT US

AIMS AND OBJECTIVES :

 To achieve the excellence in academic and non-academic programmes.

 To mould students into honest and responsible citizens

 To adopt a holistic approach to education on the Christian principles of service to people.

 To install a sense of responsibility in the students and help them develop into dependable people with integrity in their chosen professions.

 To enable the students to discover and develop inherent skills through suitable Co-curricular programmes.

 To introduce activities designed to encourage co-operation and leadership among the students.

 To offer courses in keeping with the changing scenario of the society and the increasing demands brought on by globalization of the country.
